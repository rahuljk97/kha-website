A Few Words About Us

Kerala Hindus of Arizona (KHA) is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting Kerala's rich heritage, customs, and traditions. Through diverse cultural activities, educational programs, social gatherings, and humanitarian efforts, KHA connects the community and encourages the younger generation to appreciate and take pride in their Kerala roots. We invite you to join us and participate in our community outreach activities.
